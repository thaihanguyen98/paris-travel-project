Why Paris?

Paris, the city of love

Paris, France's capital, is a major European city and a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

CAFÉS

My favorite cafe in Paris

Café Saint-Medard

Classic bistro fare at a stylish restaurant with a covered terrace overlooking a fountain.

Address

53 Rue Censier, 75005 Paris, France

What I like about it

Set in a cozy square, this brasserie has everything that you could ask for a pleasant lunch in an authentic Parisian atmosphere.

Violetta et Alfredo

Quaint tearoom with breakfast & brunch plates, cakes & an extensive tea menu, plus sidewalk tables.

Address

30 Rue de Trévise, 75009 Paris, France

What I like about it

Such a cute spot! The decor is particular and the food was just amazing. Portions are big and fluffy pancakes where just delicious. It was difficult to finish but had to, it was too good!

Boulangerie Murciano

Elegant counter-serve stop presenting delicate French & Ashkenazi pastries, plus bread & coffee.

Address

16 Rue des Rosiers, 75004 Paris, France

What I like about it

Amazing boulangerie, everything we bought was very delicious. The shopkeeper is very nice and helpful, there's a wide variety of things to choose from.
